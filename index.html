About Me

My name is John McArthur. I am a junior at Babson College near Boston, MA. My favorite hobby is playing baseball, as I play for Babson and wear #24. My favorite baseball team is the St. Louis Cardinals, as I am from St. Louis, MO, and I am a diehard fan. I love to watch as many games as I can and follow the team closely. I also like to play golf and go to the gym in my free time.

My journey at Babson has been great. I came to Babson after I graduated high school in 2022 as I had the opportunity to play baseball and also study Finance, which I knew I wanted to do before coming here. So far at Babson, I've made great progress in my concentration in Finance and Economics, and I've also explored other opportunities through groups like the Babson Finance Association.

I have also discovered an interest in Computer Science, and I have really enjoyed learning skills like Python, SQLite, HTML, and CSS. In looking towards the future, I plan on working in Finance, as I have really enjoyed learning and exploring it. Some more specific fields I am interested in are Private Equity, Venture Capital, and Wealth Management.

An interesting fact about me is that I am the 4th generation John McArthur in my family, and I am very proud of that. In school, I love to learn about Finance, Computer Science, and Economics. Those types of classes are my favorite, as I always have enjoyed and preferred to work with numbers.

My Interests

My biggest interest and passion is baseball. I have been playing since I was about five years old and I still love playing today. I also love following professional baseball, especially the St. Louis Cardinals, while learning about players and teams as much as I can.

Another interest of mine is listening to music. My favorite genre is country and some of my favorite artists are Riley Green, Cody Johnson, Chris Stapleton, and Sam Barber. I have seen Riley Green and Chris Stapleton live in concert before, and both were phenomenal!
